AtCoder.Internal.Barrett

Description

Fast modular multiplication by barrett reduction. Reference: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Barrett_reduction

Example

>>> let bt = new32 10 -- mod 10
>>> umod bt
10
>>> mulMod bt 7 7
9
