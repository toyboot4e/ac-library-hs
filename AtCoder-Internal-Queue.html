AtCoder.Internal.Queue

Description

Queue with fixed size capacity.

>>> que <- new @_ @Int 3
>>> capacity que
3
>>> pushBack que 0
>>> pushBack que 1
>>> pushBack que 2
>>> length que
3
>>> popFront que
Just 0
>>> freeze que
[1,2]
>>> pushFront que 10
>>> pushFront que 1000
*** Exception: AtCoder.Internal.Queue.pushFront: no empty front space
...
>>> unsafeFreeze que
[10,1,2]
>>> clear que
>>> pushBack que 0
>>> pushBack que 1
>>> pushBack que 2
>>> freeze que
[0,1,2]
